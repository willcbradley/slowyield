The doctors I admire understand things deeply.

They grapple with concepts that aren't always "high yield" for exams, but that make all the difference in the long run.

SlowYield is my attempt to emulate them.

It's structured as an educational resource (since teaching aids understanding), comprised of bare-bones articles (to maximise iteration speed), and hosted online (so I can access it from any hospital computer).

Please read the disclaimer if you haven't already.

And I'm always looking for smart, hungry, long-term people. If that sounds like you, let me know.

- Will Bradley
